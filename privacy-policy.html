Introduction

Welcome to Sugar-Free 21 Days ("we," "our," or "the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

üåü Key Point: Your privacy is our priority. All your data stays on your device, and we do not collect or share any personal information with third parties.

Information We Collect

1. Information Stored Locally

The Sugar-Free 21 Days app stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Progress Data: Your daily sugar-free tracking records, streak counts, and challenge completion status
  • User Preferences: App settings, notification preferences, and customization options
  • Achievement Data: Badges earned and milestones reached
  • Journal Entries: Any notes or reflections you add (if applicable)

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we don't collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, address, phone number)
  • Email addresses (unless you voluntarily contact us)
  • Location data
  • Health or medical information
  • Payment information (handled securely by Apple/Google)
  • Device information or analytics
  • Usage tracking or behavioral data

How We Use Your Information

Since all data is stored locally on your device, we use it solely to:

  1. Provide App Functionality: Track your progress, calculate streaks, and display your achievements
  2. Personalize Your Experience: Remember your settings and preferences
  3. Send Notifications: Remind you to log your progress (only if you enable notifications)
  4. Improve the App: All improvements are based on user feedback, not data collection

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

All your data is stored exclusively on your device using secure local storage mechanisms provided by your operating system (iOS/Android). This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • We cannot access your data
  • No servers or cloud storage are used for your personal information
  • Your data is protected by your device's security features

Data Backup

If you use your device's backup features (iCloud for iOS or Google Drive for Android), your app data may be included in those backups. These backups are controlled by Apple or Google, respectively, and are subject to their privacy policies.

Third-Party Services

App Store / Google Play

The app is distributed through Apple App Store and Google Play Store. Any information collected during the download process is governed by Apple's and Google's respective privacy policies.

In-App Purchases

If you make in-app purchases, payment processing is handled entirely by Apple or Google. We do not have access to your payment information. These transactions are subject to Apple's and Google's privacy policies.

No Advertising or Analytics

We do not use third-party advertising networks or analytics services. There are no trackers, cookies, or similar technologies in our app.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since all data is stored locally and we don't collect any information, children can safely use the app under parental guidance.

Your Rights and Choices

Data Control

Since your data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app at any time
  • Delete: You can reset your progress or delete all data through the app settings
  • Export: Currently, data export features may be limited (check app for latest features)
  • Uninstall: Deleting the app will remove all locally stored data

Notifications

You can enable or disable push notifications at any time through:

  • The app's settings menu
  • Your device's system settings

Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • We will notify you through the app or on this page
  • Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
